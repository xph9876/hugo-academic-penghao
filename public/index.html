Post-doc Fellow

Georgia Institute of Technology

I’m a post-doc fellow at Storici Lab. I earned my Ph.D. in Bioinformatics at Georgia Tech in 2022, with the advising of Dr. Francesca Storici. Before that, I received the M.S. in Computational Sciences and Engineering at Georgia Tech in 2019 and B.Sc. in Biological Sciences at Fudan University in 2017. My research include rNMP incorporation, DNA replication, and Nanopore sequencing.
